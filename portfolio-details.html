Campus Recruitment System

This was an individual project. There are three modules in this poject viz. Admin(College), Students and Companies. This website is best suited for pandemic like situations where students can directly check the websites for their campus interviews. Also students can apply for jobs, upload their resume, edit their profile and the website also provides a section for students to check where they have applied. The company can post job vacancies, see the number of students who have applied for the job and their uploaded files and can also send them an email. The admin can view all students and company profile and also edit it. The admin can also see the posted vacancy and edit it.

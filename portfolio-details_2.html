Oxygen Cylinder Track and Trace using NFC

Our group implemented an automated management system which uses NFC technology and RFID tags to track and trace the Oxygen cylinders manufactured in a factory and also its proper delivery to the hospitals in need of it. The system has three modules viz. Webapp for factory, Webapp for hospital and Android app for truck drivers. I worked on front end and 50% backend for factory and hospital webapps. This system was developed to track the loading of oxygen cylinders and proper maintenance of data through a single database. The distribution of oxygen cylinders to hospitals is done on priority basis.
